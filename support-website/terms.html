2. Description of Service

Seedlock is a secure mnemonic phrase management application that allows you to:

  • Store and manage cryptocurrency mnemonic phrases
  • Generate BIP-39 compliant mnemonic phrases
  • Encrypt data locally on your device
  • Optionally sync encrypted data via iCloud
  • Create secure backups of your data

The App operates offline by default. When iCloud sync is enabled, only encrypted data is transmitted via Apple CloudKit. Your mnemonic phrases are encrypted before leaving your device, and we have no access to your data.
