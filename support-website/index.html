üîí Is my data safe?

Yes. Seedlock uses military-grade AES-256-GCM encryption and a zero-knowledge architecture. This means:

  • Your data is encrypted on your device before syncing
  • We never have access to your encryption keys
  • Even we cannot read your recovery phrases
  • Only you can decrypt your data
üîë What if I forget my password?

Seedlock uses your device's biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID), so you don't need to remember a password. However:

  • Make sure biometric authentication is set up on your device
  • Keep regular backups of your data
  • If you lose access to your device, restore from iCloud backup on a new device
‚òÅÔ∏è Does Seedlock store my data on servers?

Only if you enable iCloud Sync. When enabled:

  • Data is encrypted on your device BEFORE syncing
  • We only store encrypted data - we cannot read it
  • Your encryption keys never leave your device
  • Data is stored in your personal iCloud account

If you don't enable iCloud Sync, all data stays only on your device.
