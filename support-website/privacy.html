Overview

Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how SeedLock handles your data with industry-leading encryption and zero-knowledge architecture.

1. Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Seedlock is designed with a zero-knowledge architecture, ensuring complete privacy:

  • Your mnemonic phrases are encrypted locally
  • Your data remains private at all times
  • Only you can access your data
  • All encryption happens on your device

2. Data We Don't Collect

Seedlock does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • ❌ Your mnemonic phrases
  • ❌ Your passwords or backup passwords
  • ❌ Your personal information
  • ❌ Your usage patterns
  • ❌ Analytics or tracking data
  • ❌ Location data

3. Local Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device:

  • ✅ Encrypted with AES-256-GCM
  • ✅ Stored in iOS Keychain
  • ✅ Never leaves your device (unless you enable iCloud)
  • ✅ Only accessible with device authentication

4. Optional iCloud Sync

If you choose to enable iCloud sync:

  • Data is end-to-end encrypted before syncing
  • Synced via Apple's CloudKit service
  • Apple's privacy policy applies to iCloud data
  • You can disable sync at any time
  • Synced data remains encrypted

5. Third-Party Services

Seedlock uses the following Apple services:

  • iOS Keychain (for secure local storage)
  • CloudKit (optional, for encrypted sync)
  • iCloud Drive (optional, for backups)

These services are governed by Apple's privacy policy.

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • ✅ Export your data at any time
  • ✅ Delete your data at any time
  • ✅ Disable iCloud sync at any time
  • ✅ No account required
  • ✅ No vendor lock-in

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app with an updated "Last Updated" date.
